Mission

AI should adapt to humans.

H/AI is an independent initiative exploring technologies, systems, and authoring environments that make AI interaction more natural, continuous, contextual, and human.

The focus is not replacing people. The focus is amplifying them.

Current Focus

Where the work is happening now.

Conversational Runtime Systems

Persistent conversational state, interruption recovery, contextual continuity, branch restoration, and multimodal orchestration.

AI Authoring

Exploring what a modern authoring environment for AI-native interactive experiences could become for non-technical creators.

Educational Assessment

Conversational systems for oral defense, adaptive questioning, evidence-of-understanding, transcript capture, and instructor review.

H/AI Language

A developing runtime vocabulary for human-centered AI interaction, traversal, orchestration, context, intent, state, event, and action.
